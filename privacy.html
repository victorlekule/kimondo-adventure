Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Kimondo Adventure, we deeply respect your right to privacy. We are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us when you browse our website, make a booking, or communicate with our team. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, disclosure, and protection of your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws. By using our services, you consent to the practices described here.

2

Information We Collect

  • Identity & Contact: Full name, passport number, nationality, date of birth, email, phone, address.
  • Travel & Health: Dietary requirements, medical conditions, allergies, travel insurance, emergency contacts.
  • Payment Data: Card numbers, bank references, billing addresses (we never store full card details).
  • Technical & Usage: IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring URLs (via cookies).
3

How We Use Your Information

  • Process & confirm your tour/safari/trek bookings.
  • Communicate itinerary changes, pre‑departure info.
  • Arrange permits, flights, accommodations via third parties.
  • Customer support, complaints, and emergency assistance.
  • Anonymous analytics to improve our services.
  • Marketing emails – only with your explicit consent (unsubscribe anytime).
4

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We never sell your data. We share only what’s necessary with trusted partners:

  • Hotels, lodges, airlines – to secure reservations.
  • Local guides, ground operators – smooth tour execution.
  • Payment processors, banks – to handle transactions.
  • Authorities when required by law.

All parties are contractually bound to protect your data.

5

How We Protect Your Data

We use SSL encryption, secure servers, access controls, and regular security audits. While no system is 100% foolproof, we diligently protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience, analyze traffic, and remember preferences. You can disable cookies via your browser, though some features may not work properly. We use anonymized Google Analytics data; no personal identifiers are stored in cookies.

7

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined here, or as required by law (typically 7 years for financial records). After that, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

8

Your Data Rights

  • Access – request a copy of your data.
  • Rectification – correct inaccurate info.
  • Erasure – deletion when no longer needed.
  • Restrict processing – limit usage.
  • Portability – receive data in common format.
  • Objection – object to processing for marketing.

Email privacy@kimondoadventure.com – we’ll respond within 30 days.

9

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at minors under 18 without a guardian. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us for immediate deletion.

10

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your home country (e.g., Tanzania). We ensure adequate safeguards (standard contractual clauses) to protect your privacy.

11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date. Continued use of our services after revisions constitutes acceptance.
