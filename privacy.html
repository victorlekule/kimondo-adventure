Information We Collect

  • Identity & Contact Information – Full name, email address, phone/WhatsApp number, nationality, and country of residence.
  • Booking & Travel Details – Travel dates, group size, accommodation preferences, dietary or medical needs relevant to your safari, trek, or tour, and passport details where required for park permits or travel documentation.
  • Payment Information – When you make a booking, payment details are collected and processed securely by our payment and booking platform provider. Kimondo does not store full card numbers on its own systems.
  • Automatically Collected Information – IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing behavior on our website, collected through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Communication Records – Messages sent via our website contact form, WhatsApp, email, or through our booking platform.
3

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and confirm bookings for safaris, tours, treks, volunteering programs, and shop purchases.
  • Verify identity and prevent fraud in connection with bookings and payments.
  • Communicate with you before, during, and after your trip.
  • Arrange necessary travel documentation and entry requirements (e.g., ZIC travel insurance, park permits).
  • Improve our website, services, and customer experience.
  • Send marketing communications – only if you've opted in (travel tips or promotional offers).
  • Comply with legal, tax, and regulatory obligations in Tanzania and Zanzibar.
4

Payment Processing & Our Booking Platform Partner

To securely process bookings and payments, Kimondo Adventures uses Tab (Tab.travel), a third‑party booking and payment platform built for tourism businesses. When you make a booking or payment through our website, your relevant identity, contact, and payment information is shared with Tab solely to complete and secure your transaction.

Tab is independently responsible for the security of payment data under applicable card industry standards (PCI‑DSS) and maintains its own privacy policy governing how it handles your payment information, available at tab.travel. Kimondo does not have access to, and does not store, your full card details.

5

Cookies & Website Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how visitors use our site and to improve your browsing experience. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings; note that some site features, including booking functionality, may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

6

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with:

  • Tab (Tab.travel) – Our Booking & Payment Platform Provider – To process and secure your booking and payment (see Section 4).
  • Service Partners – Accommodation providers, transport partners, park authorities, and licensed guides directly involved in delivering your booked experience.
  • Legal & Regulatory Authorities – Where required by Tanzanian or Zanzibari law, such as immigration or park permit requirements.
  • Service Providers – Trusted vendors who support our website, email, or booking systems, bound by confidentiality obligations.

We do not share your information with third parties for their own independent marketing purposes.

7

International Visitors & Data Transfers

As we serve travelers from around the world, your information may be processed and stored in Tanzania, or in the countries where Tab (our booking and payment platform provider) or other service providers operate. Where data is transferred internationally, we and Tab take steps to ensure it is protected to a standard consistent with applicable data protection law, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where relevant. By booking with us, you consent to this transfer and processing, which is necessary to deliver your travel arrangements.

8

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy – including completing your booking, providing customer support, complying with legal and tax obligations, resolving disputes, and preventing fraud. Booking and transaction records are typically retained for a minimum of 5–7 years to meet financial and legal record‑keeping requirements in Tanzania.

9

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse, and we rely on Tab's PCI‑DSS‑compliant payment processing for all payment card data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal, tax, or contractual retention requirements.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Object to certain uses of your data.
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority in your country of residence.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 14.
