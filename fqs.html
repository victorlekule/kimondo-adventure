General & Booking

How far in advance should I book my trip?

For safaris and Kilimanjaro treks during peak season (June–October and December–March), we recommend booking 2–3 months in advance to secure guides, vehicles, and accommodation availability. Zanzibar tours and shorter experiences can often be arranged with less notice.

Can you build a fully custom itinerary, or do I have to choose a package?

Every Kimondo itinerary is tailor-made. Our listed packages are starting points, not fixed templates — tell us your dates, interests, and budget, and we'll build a plan around you.

What's included in the price I'm quoted?

Quotes typically include guiding, transport, park fees where applicable, and specified accommodation or meals — but this varies by trip. We break down exactly what's included and excluded before you commit, so there are no surprises later.

Do you only work with international travelers, or can locals book too?

Both. We work with international travelers seeking full East African journeys as well as local and regional clients booking individual experiences.

Safaris

What's the best time of year for a safari?

It depends on your priority. December–March is calving season in the southern Serengeti; July–October offers dramatic Mara River crossings. We advise based on your specific travel dates rather than a generic "best time" answer.

Is a safari safe for children or older travelers?

Yes, with the right planning. We adjust pace, vehicle choice, and daily schedules based on who's traveling — families and older travelers are common on our safaris.

How many people will be in my safari vehicle?

This depends on the package. Private safaris mean your vehicle only, while joined/group safaris may include other travelers to reduce cost. We'll clarify which applies before you book.

Zanzibar & Island Tours

Do I need travel insurance to enter Zanzibar?

Yes. All visitors are required to hold ZIC (Zanzibar Insurance Corporation) travel insurance, currently priced at $44 per person, separate from any personal travel insurance you already have. We help guests handle this smoothly on arrival.

Is clear kayaking suitable for beginners?

Yes. It requires no prior kayaking or diving experience and takes place in calm, shallow lagoon waters under guide supervision — suitable for most ages and skill levels.

Can you combine Zanzibar with a Tanzania mainland safari?

Yes — this is one of our most requested combinations. Since we handle both under one team, transitions between the island and mainland are coordinated seamlessly within a single itinerary.

Kilimanjaro & Trekking

Do I need prior climbing experience to summit Kilimanjaro?

No technical climbing skill is required — Kilimanjaro is a trek, not a technical climb. Physical fitness and proper acclimatization matter far more than prior experience.

Which route has the highest success rate?

Routes with more acclimatization days, such as Machame or Lemosho, generally have higher summit success rates than shorter routes like Marangu, regardless of a climber's fitness level.

What happens if I get altitude sickness during the climb?

Our certified guides continuously monitor climbers for altitude sickness symptoms and carry supplemental oxygen and monitoring equipment. Guide judgment on pacing — or turning back if necessary — always takes priority over reaching the summit on a fixed schedule.
