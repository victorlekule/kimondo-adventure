Not every journey with Kimondo is about wildlife sightings and sunset dhow cruises. Why Kimondo

Real Impact, Not Performative Tourism

Plenty of "volunteer travel" programs exist to sell you a feel-good photo, not to create lasting change. Kimondo takes a different approach. Every program is built in partnership with the local communities and conservation groups it supports — designed around what they actually need, not what looks good on social media.

Because we're the same locally-run team behind your safari and Zanzibar bookings, you get the same standard of safety, planning, and on-the-ground support — just applied to a program built around contribution instead of consumption.

Our Programs

Two Ways to Get Involved

01

Community & Conservation Volunteering

Join grassroots programs across Tanzania and Zanzibar — from environmental conservation and wildlife protection initiatives to education and community development projects. Placements range from short-term (1–2 weeks) to extended stays for travelers who want deeper immersion.

What you might work on:

  • Environmental & wildlife conservation
  • Teaching and youth education
  • Community infrastructure & development
  • Coastal and marine conservation
02

Internships in Tourism & Hospitality

Gain real, hands-on experience in East Africa's tourism industry — working alongside our safari planning, guest experience, and operations teams. Ideal for students and early-career professionals building a global portfolio.

What you might gain:

  • Direct mentorship from travel professionals
  • Real operational experience — not shadowing
  • A ground-level view of East African tourism
