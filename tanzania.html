Best Time to Visit

Timing Shapes Your Safari —
Honest Breakdown

Each season offers a unique window into Tanzania's wildlife drama. Here's what to expect, month by month.

Peak Season

June – October

Dry season – peak wildlife viewing, Mara River crossings (Jul‑Oct), and endless sunny days. The classic safari experience.

Best for Great Migration & Big Cats
Calving Season

December – March

Green landscapes, newborn wildebeest, and dramatic predator action – a photographer's dream.

Lush Scenery & Baby Animals
Green Season

April – May

Lower rates, lush scenery, and fewer crowds – a serene, budget‑friendly window.

Best Value & Quiet Parks
Transition

November

Short rains begin, landscapes turn green again, and the migration moves south – a quiet, beautiful shift.
